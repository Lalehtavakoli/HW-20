Wins DC Website Redesign case study

Problem Statement

Olivia, a recently retired public school teacher, would like to contribute her time and money to a charitable cause whose mission focuses on reading literacy issues in children within her community. Everybody Wins, DC’s programs strengthen literacy skills for children through reading and book programs, but it’s difficult to find information on their website about their mentoring and book donation programs.
