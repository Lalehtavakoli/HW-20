Wins DC Website Redesign Case Study

Our team redesigned the EWDC website with this goal in mind. We believe that making the mentoring and book donation programs more prominent on the homepage of the EWDC website will attract more donors and volunteers who are passionate in changing the lives of local children. We did this by creating three designated buttons, each representing the main ways one can contribute to the nonprofit’s mission.This allowed for users to immediately navigate to pages containing mentoring and book donation information.
